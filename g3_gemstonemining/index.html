Gemstone Mining Instructions
  • You are one of Snow White's Seven Dwarves on a quest for gems!
  • You have two choices: Mine for gems (left/doorway button) or exit the mine(right/'Exit' button)
  • If you get a diamond, an emerald, a ruby, or a topaz gem, you get points. If you get an obsidian (the black gem), you get no points.
  • But beware! If you get two obsidians, you will die and won't keep any of your gems or points.
  • If you choose to leave, you will keep your gems and points but won't be able to mine.
  • Choose wisely!
